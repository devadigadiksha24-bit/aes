AES Encryption Demo

Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) is a symmetric encryption algorithm widely used to secure data. With this demo, you can encrypt and decrypt text directly in your browser safely and instantly.

Why AES?

  • In 1990's the cracking of DES algorithm became possible.
  • Around 50 hours of brute-forcing allowed messages to be cracked.
  • NIST started searching for a new feasible algorithm and proposed its requirements in 1997.
  • In 2001, Rijndael algorithm designed by Rijmen and Daemen of Belgium was declared as the winner.
  • It met all security, cost, and implementation criteria.
